Classic Margherita Pizza

A Margherita pizza is a timeless classic, known for its simple yet flavorful combination of fresh mozzarella, tangy tomato sauce, and fragrant basil leaves. This pizza is not only quick and easy to make, but it's also a crowd-pleaser. Whether you're making it for a family dinner or a casual gathering, it's sure to be a hit. The crisp golden crust and melted cheese provide a delightful texture and taste. You can make it with store-bought dough or go the extra mile by preparing homemade dough. This dish is perfect for pizza lovers who appreciate the beauty of simplicity. Enjoy it with a drizzle of olive oil for added richness.

prep time

15 min.

cook time

10-12 min.

serving

4 servings

Tags : beef breakfast pancakes food

instructions

step 1

Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).

step 2

Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface and transfer it to a baking sheet.

step 3

Spread pizza sauce over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.

step 4

Add slices of mozzarella cheese on top of the sauce

step 5

Season with salt and pepper and drizzle with olive oil

step 6

Bake for 10-12 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.

step 7

Remove from the oven and top with fresh basil leaves before serving.

ingredients

1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)

1/2 cup pizza sauce

1 1/2 cups fresh mozzarella, sliced

Fresh Basil Leaves

Olive Oil

Salt and pepper to taste

tools

Rolling Pin

Pizza Stone or Baking Sheet

Knife

Oven
